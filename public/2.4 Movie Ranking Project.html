The Best Anime According to Anton

My Top 5 Anime Of All-Time.

#1. Sword Art Online

Firstly this anime is an isekai. I love isekais. SAO made me go through a whirlwind of emotions.

#2. One Piece

This anime is unfinishable as it has over a thousand episodes filled with great stories and character development.

#3. Black Clover

I love the characters, the plot, and the fan service.

#4. Tensei Shittara Slime Datta Ken

Another isekai great but this time with politics and empire building set in a fantasy world.

#5. Naruto

Amazing out-of-the-box stories with child ninjas who grow in to adults over the years
